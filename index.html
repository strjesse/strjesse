Questions?

What exactly happens in the 90 minutes?+

Jesse walks you through how the rental arbitrage model works and maps it onto your situation — your market, budget, and goals. Bring questions; the more specific, the more you'll get out of it. You leave with a written action plan.

Do I need to own property?+

No. Rental arbitrage means you lease (or manage) a unit with the owner's permission and operate it as a short-term rental. Zero ownership, zero mortgage — that's the whole point of the model.

I'm a complete beginner — is this for me?+

Especially. The session is built to teach the model from the ground up. We'll also make sure arbitrage actually fits your situation before you spend a dollar building anything — and if it doesn't, you'll hear that honestly.
