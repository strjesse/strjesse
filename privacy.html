Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

This policy explains how Jesse Height, trading as STRJesse (a sole trader based in Victoria, Australia), handles your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Information we collect

We only collect information reasonably necessary to provide our mentoring service. This may include:

  • Identity and contact details — your name and email address.
  • Booking details — your chosen session date and time, and the responses you provide in the pre-session questionnaire (such as your market, budget and goals).
  • Payment information — payments are processed by Stripe. We receive confirmation that a payment succeeded, but we do not collect or store your full card details.
  • Session materials — a recording and/or summary of your session, and any information you share during it.

2. How we collect it

We collect information directly from you when you book a session, complete the questionnaire, email us, or attend your session. Where practicable, you may deal with us anonymously, though we generally need your name and email to deliver the service.

3. Why we use it

  • To schedule, confirm and deliver your session;
  • To send you calendar invites, confirmations, meeting links and the resources included with your session;
  • To tailor the session to your situation;
  • To respond to your enquiries and manage our relationship with you; and
  • To meet our legal, tax and record-keeping obligations.

4. Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate, including:

  • Stripe — payment processing;
  • Google — Google Calendar and Google Meet (scheduling and the video call) and Gmail (sending your confirmation);
  • Google Forms — the pre-session questionnaire;
  • Vercel — website hosting.

5. Overseas disclosure

Some of these providers store or process data outside Australia (for example, in the United States). By using our service you consent to your information being handled by these providers under their own privacy terms.

6. Security and retention

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from misuse, loss and unauthorised access. We keep your information only for as long as needed to provide the service and to meet our legal obligations, after which we take reasonable steps to delete or de-identify it.

7. Your rights

You may request access to, or correction of, the personal information we hold about you by emailing info@strjesse.com.au. If you have a complaint about how we handle your information, contact us first; if it is not resolved, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

8. Cookies and analytics

Our website does not currently set tracking or advertising cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be available on this page, with the "last updated" date shown above.

10. Contact

Jesse Height, trading as STRJesse — info@strjesse.com.au.
